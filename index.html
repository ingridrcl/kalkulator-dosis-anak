💧 Cairan Maintenance — Holiday-Segar
📖 Rumus Holiday-Segar
Kebutuhan per hari:
• 0–10 kg → 100 ml/kg/hari
• 10–20 kg → 1000 ml + 50 ml/kg tiap kg di atas 10
• >20 kg → 1500 ml + 20 ml/kg tiap kg di atas 20

Rumus 4-2-1 (ml/jam):
• 0–10 kg → 4 ml/kg/jam
• 10–20 kg → 40 ml/jam + 2 ml/kg/jam di atas 10
• >20 kg → 60 ml/jam + 1 ml/kg/jam di atas 20

📚 Holiday MA, Segar WE. Pediatrics 1957 · Formularium Nasional RI
